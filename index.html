London Public Transport Injury Dashboard

Intro:This interactive dashboard shows all the injuries happened during a period of time in London public transport through charts. Bar charts are employed to compare data by boroughs, type of incidents and operators. Line Charts are used to show the number of incidents over time and season. Finally, Treemap is employed to relate different segments of data to the whole dataset.
